🌟 1. Our Commitment to Accessibility ♿

At Dominguez Tech Solutions, we are dedicated to providing a website that is inclusive and accessible to all users, regardless of their abilities. 🌍 Our goal is to ensure that everyone can seamlessly browse our website and access our services without barriers. 🤝

🔧 2. Accessibility Standards

This website has been designed to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines help ensure that our content is accessible to individuals with disabilities, including those with blindness, low vision, hearing loss, or cognitive impairments. 🌐

✨ 3. Features of Our Accessible Website

  • 🖼️ Text alternatives for all non-text content.
  • 🎹 Keyboard navigation for all interactive elements.
  • 🎨 High contrast ratios for improved readability.
  • 📱 Responsive design that adapts to different devices and assistive technologies.
